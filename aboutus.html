About Us

We are an avid team of determined, young entrepreneurs building an inviting experience for friends and family to enjoy time together in Southeast Florida.

Luke Burgess - CEO

Luke Burgess is the CEO of Beach Bowls. At 35 years old, he holds the responsibility of finding an optimal business location, including it’s cost, and also ensuring a clean, safe entertainment venue, directing workers, and managing task status.

Before dreaming of a fun, upscale environment for people to have a chance to relax and have fun in the midst of a life of busyness, Luke was an assistant manager of a popular, local restaurant. Starting work experience at the young age of 15 was not problem for Luke, and he was working his first job as a golf caddy at a golf course in his hometown. By age 24, he earned a degree in business administration and economics at UNC while winning many awards being a professional bowler. His other awards won are Small Business of the Year and Startup of the Year award. Lastly, his favorite hobbies are fishing, soccer, lacrosse, and of course, bowling.

Iris Pascual - General Manager

Iris Pascual is the general manager of Beach Bowls. Before rolling into the bowling business, Iris was a manager of iHop at the age of 23. Starting work experience at the young age of nineteen was not problem for Iris, and she began working her first job as a Chuck E. Cheese mascot in her hometown. By the age of 24, she earned a degree in business and economics from the University of Pennsylvania. At the age of 28, she enjoys skydiving, playing the guitar, scuba diving, and sleeping.

Paul D'Alessandro - Head Chef

Paul D’Alessandro is the executive chef of Beach Bowl. He chooses the foods on the menu, and cooks them too. He first worked at a restaurant called Cenzino, but then left to work at Beach Bowl. He graduated from the Culinary Institute of America at 23 years old. Paul later went on a competitive cooking show, and won $25,000. Now, 28 years old, he works at an incredible restaurant called Beach Bowl.

Olivia de los Reyes - Vice President of Marketing and Advertising

Olivia de los Reyes is the Vice President of marketing and advertising at Beach Bowl. Currently, she is 28 years old. Most recently, she was employed to work as the V.P. of marketing and advertising at Amazon from when she was 24 up until about 6 months ago, when she started working at Beach Bowl. Right after she graduated from the University of Florida with her bachelor's degree in marketing, she got her first job working as the marketer for Youtube Ads when she was 24, but then quickly switched over to Amazon, and then later to Beach Bowl. When she was 27, she was awarded with the Effie Award, which is awarded to the best marketer of the year. In her free time, Olivia enjoys swimming, rock climbing, parasailing, and enjoying her time at Beach Bowl.

Sanya Shekhar - Vice President of Finance

Sanya Shekhar is the Vice President of Finance at Beach Bowl. Currently, she is 29 years old. She went to the University of Pennsylvania and got her Masters in Finance at the age of 24. Before working as a VP at Beach Bowl, she was working as an investment banker for 5 years at Goldman Sachs. She went on to be a managing director in her position. Although she had a great job, she followed her dream to become a partial business owner. Her hobbies include sleeping, bowling, and reading.

Robbie Tanis - Vice President of Technology

Robbie Tanis is the Vice President of Technology at Beach Bowl. Before moving to sunny West Palm to devote all of his free time to managing Beach Bowl from a technological standpoint, he lived in Cambridge, Massachusetts while attending MIT for a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Engineering and working a part time job at a local Target. Later, at age 24, he graduated from Harbour.Space University in Barcelona, Spain with a Master’s Degree in Data Science. After graduating from Harbour.Space, he moved back to the United States and got a job at Duolingo as a Web Designer and Machine Learning Engineer. After getting a call from his middle school friends Luke, Sanya, Iris, Olivia, and Paul about a new bowling alley restaurant startup, he knew he had to pounce on the opportunity. Although he still works with Duolingo to support Beach Bowl, he moved to West Palm Beach to spend as much time as possible working with the company, third party vendors, and his coworkers. In his spare time, he enjoys bowling, obviously, designing web programs, going to the beach, and traveling the world.
